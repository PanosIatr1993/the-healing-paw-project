OUR HISTORY

Our History

The Healing Paw was founded in 1990 by Dr. Sarah Johnson, a passionate animal lover who wanted to provide top-quality veterinary care to pets in the local community. Dr. Johnson had a vision of creating a warm and welcoming environment where pet owners could feel comfortable bringing their furry friends for routine check-ups, as well as more complex medical procedures. Over the years, the Healing Paw has become known for its personalized care and exceptional service, with a team of highly skilled veterinarians and support staff dedicated to helping animals lead healthy and happy lives.


Our Values

At the Healing Paw, we believe that every animal deserves the best possible care, and we are committed to providing compassionate and comprehensive veterinary services to pets and their owners. Our core values include:

  1. Compassion: We understand that pets are beloved family members, and we treat each animal with the same care and respect that we would want for our own pets.
  2. Excellence: We are committed to delivering the highest standard of veterinary care, using state-of-the-art equipment and the latest medical techniques.
  3. Integrity: We believe in honesty and transparency, and we always communicate openly with our clients about their pet's health and treatment options.
  4. Education: We are dedicated to educating pet owners about the importance of preventative care, and we provide resources and support to help pet owners make informed decisions about their pet's health.
  5. Community: We are proud to be a part of the local community, and we are committed to giving back through volunteer work and supporting local animal rescue organizations.

Overall, the Healing Paw is dedicated to providing exceptional veterinary care with a focus on compassion, excellence, integrity, education, and community.
