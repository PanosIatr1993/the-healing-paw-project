OUR STAFF

Our friendly and supportive staff genuinely cares for your four-legged companions. You will often see the same smiling faces each time you visit! Our comprehensive approach to pet health involves a team of talented and caring animal lovers devoted to your pet's specific health needs.

  • Dr. Sofia Johnson: Sarah's daughter and co-owner, who joined the practice in 2005 after completing her veterinary degree. She is known for her expertise in surgery and her compassionate approach to animal care.
